Reply Pilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Reply Pilot is a Shopify embedded app for merchant review-reply workflows. This policy explains what data the app may process, how it is used, and how merchants and shoppers can request access or deletion.

Data Read From Shopify

Reply Pilot uses Shopify API access only for the scopes approved by the merchant during installation. Depending on enabled features, Reply Pilot may read shop domain, app session data, product information, and review or workflow context supplied by the merchant or connected services.

Data Sent By Merchants

Merchants may submit support requests, customization requests, configuration values, brand voice guidance, and other operational text entered in the app. Reply Pilot stores these records to provide support, maintain settings, and operate the app.

Shopper Data

Reply Pilot is not designed to store Shopify customer account records. If a merchant connects review workflows, shopper review text, customer names, ratings, product references, or reply drafts may be processed so the merchant can manage replies. Reply Pilot does not sell shopper data.

Use Of Data

Data is used to authenticate the app, provide embedded app features, support merchants, improve app reliability, respond to privacy requests, and comply with Shopify platform requirements.

Retention

App sessions and merchant-entered data are retained while the app is installed or while needed for support, security, legal compliance, and operational records. Shop data is deleted when required by Shopify privacy webhooks or upon verified merchant request.

Providers

Reply Pilot uses Shopify for platform services, PostgreSQL for app data storage, Docker hosting infrastructure, and SMTP email providers configured by the app operator for support messages. If Gmail SMTP is used, the SMTP password must be a Gmail App Password.

Security

Reply Pilot validates Shopify authentication and webhooks, uses HTTPS in production, stores secrets outside version control, and limits database access per app with a dedicated database user.

Your Rights

Merchants and shoppers may request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal data. Shopper requests should include the shop name and enough information for the merchant and app operator to locate the relevant records.

Privacy Webhooks

Reply Pilot implements Shopify mandatory privacy webhooks for customers/data_request, customers/redact, and shop/redact. The shop redact webhook deletes persisted data for the affected shop.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, contact the app operator at the email configured as CONTACT_EMAIL in production.
